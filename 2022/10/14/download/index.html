Free Air

This is a mobile app focusing on respiratory health, which can automatically locate users and detect local air quality. App can automatically generate suggestions on protective measures for different populations according to the air quality level. It also has a map function, which can automatically generate the nearest hospital route and estimated duration in case of emergency.
